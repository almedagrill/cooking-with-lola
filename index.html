Cooking with Lola — Learn Tagalog Food Words

Learn Tagalog the fun way by cooking authentic Filipino dishes! Cooking with Lola is a free interactive game that teaches you 100+ Tagalog food vocabulary words through 20 classic Filipino recipes.

How It Works

Lola (grandmother in Tagalog) guides you through each recipe. Drag the correct Tagalog ingredients into the kawali (wok) to cook dishes like Adobo, Sinigang, Lumpia, and Halo-Halo. Tap any ingredient to hear its Tagalog pronunciation.

20 Filipino Recipes to Master

  • Main Dishes: Adobo, Sinigang, Pancit Canton, Tinola, Kare-Kare, Lumpia, Bistek Tagalog, Pinakbet, Bicol Express, Kaldereta, Mechado, Laing, Tokwa't Baboy
  • Soups: Arroz Caldo, Bulalo
  • Snacks: Lugaw, Turon
  • Desserts: Halo-Halo, Leche Flan, Champorado

Tagalog Words You'll Learn

Manok (chicken), Bawang (garlic), Toyo (soy sauce), Suka (vinegar), Baboy (pork), Kamatis (tomato), Sibuyas (onion), Kangkong (water spinach), Luya (ginger), Gata (coconut milk), Sili (chili), Talong (eggplant), Baka (beef), Patatas (potato), Bigas (rice), Itlog (egg), Saging (banana), Asukal (sugar), Gatas (milk), Tsokolate (chocolate), and many more.
